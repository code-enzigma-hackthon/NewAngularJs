Welcome to the All New Member Portal

In order for you to use the new member portal, you will need to reregister your account.

How to Register Your Account

Registering your account is easy and safe.


Step One: Click the Join Now link on the login page.

Step Two: Enter Your Email address and Submit. If the system finds your email address, it will send you an email. If it does not, please email member services at memberservices@oceancanyon.com

Step Three: Check your email for a validation link. You may need to check your spam if you don't see your email.

Step Four: When you click the validation link, you will be able to set a new password. Once the password is set, you are done!

How to Request a Reservation

Request your next stay online.


Step One: Click the red "Request a Reservation" button on the home screen.

Step Two: Enter in all the information you need and submit. Just note that more options will appear depending on what options you choose.

Step Three: You will receive a request email just to let you know we got your request. Your request will need to be approved .



If you have any questions or issues with the new member portal, we want to hear. Please email us at memberservices@oceancanyon.com at your convience.
